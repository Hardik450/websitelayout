Curriculum Overview

Pre-Primary Education

Our Pre-Primary curriculum is designed to nurture young minds with interactive and engaging activities. It focuses on:

  • Language Development
  • Basic Math Concepts
  • Creative Arts & Music
  • Social and Emotional Growth
  • Physical Activities & Play-Based Learning

Primary Education

The Primary curriculum encourages curiosity and fundamental learning. It includes:

  • Comprehensive Language & Literature
  • Foundational Mathematics & Science
  • Hands-on Environmental Studies
  • Creative Thinking & Problem-Solving
  • Physical Education & Extracurricular Activities

Middle School Education

Middle school prepares students for deeper academic engagement. The subjects covered include:

  • Advanced Mathematics & Logical Reasoning
  • Experimental Science & Technology
  • Language & Communication Skills
  • Social Sciences & Global Awareness
  • Sports & Personality Development

High School Education

The High School curriculum prepares students for higher education and career paths. The focus areas include:

  • Specialized Subject Streams (Science, Commerce, Arts)
  • Critical Thinking & Research Skills
  • Career Guidance & Personal Development
  • Internship & Project-Based Learning
  • Sports, Arts & Cultural Enrichment
